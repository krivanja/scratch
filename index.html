SCRATCH PROGRAMMING

Scratch is a block-based coding language where children can program and share stories, games, and animation with people from all over the world. As children create with Scratch, they learn to think creatively, work collaboratively, and reason systematically.

Scratch is designed especially for young people ages 8 to 16, but people of all ages create and share with Scratch. Students are learning Math, Computer Science, Social Studies with Scratch.

The computer science concepts used in Scratch can be applied to other advanced programming languages, like Python.
