Objective

  • Interact with sprites that play sounds.

Instructions

  • Use your hands to play the drums!

Extensions

  • Click the Extensions button(at the bottom left of the screen).
  • Choose Video Sensing to add the video blocks.

Sprites

  • Choose two Sprites (Drum) and (Drum-cymbal).

Code

Click on Drum to select it, then add its code.

Click on Drum-cymbal to select it, then add its code.
