Objective

  • Add your voice or other sounds to your project.

Instructions

  • Click the green flag to start.

Sprites

  • Choose a Sprite (Champ99).

Sound

  • Click the Sounds tab.
  • Then click Record from the pop-up menu.
  • To record your voice or other Sound, click the red button.
  • If your device is unable to record, you can choose a sound instead.
  • Or, choose a sound from the library.
